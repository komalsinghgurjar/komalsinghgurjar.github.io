1. Introduction

Typist (“the App”) is an offline Optical Character Recognition (OCR) application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or tracking purposes.

All OCR processing is performed entirely on the user’s device.

3. Images and Text Processing

  • Images selected by the user are processed locally on the device.
  • Recognized text remains on the device.
  • No images or extracted text are uploaded to any server.

The App does not retain images or recognized text unless the user explicitly saves them.

4. Crash Reporting (ACRA)

The App uses ACRA (Application Crash Reports for Android) for crash reporting to improve app stability.

When a crash occurs, a crash report may be sent via email. This report may include:

  • App version
  • Android version
  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Stack trace and technical error information

No personal user data, images, or OCR content are included in crash reports. Crash reports are used solely for debugging and improving the App.

5. Internet Usage

The App does not require an internet connection for OCR functionality.

Internet access may be used only for optional features such as sending crash reports or downloading optional language data in future versions.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics services
  • Cloud-based OCR services

ACRA is used solely for crash reporting and operates within the scope described above.

7. Permissions

The App may request permissions such as:

  • Media / Storage access – to allow users to select images for OCR

Permissions are used only for their stated purpose and not for data collection.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Data Security

Since the App performs OCR locally and does not transmit user content, there is minimal risk of data exposure through external servers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as new features are added. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
